GREETING!
I’m An, often known as Aly or NKA. I’m a Vietnamese Digital Designer & Illustrator, currently a Digital Media student at RMIT University. ​I'm a sucker for creating visual art whether it's physical or digital. Experimental and Expressive are my key visuals.

HOW I STARTED
I’ve been doing video editing since 2018 and have loved it ever since. It all started when I saw an edit of my favorite show on the Internet and I thought that was the coolest thing ever and only if I could do it like them. And I did! I started on my phone and then eventually switched to my laptop to edit, and After Effects has been my best best friend throughout the journey. I love the Instagram editing community (iykyk).

MY OTHER HOBBIES
I’m also a music person. I love Alice in Wonderland live-action (love Tim Burton’s style). My favorite show is Dark. My favorite book is Wuthering Heights.
