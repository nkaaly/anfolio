GREETING!
Hi, I'm An - also known as Aly or “nka”. I'm a Digital Designer and Illustrator specialising in bold, story-driven visuals and clear, brand-aligned design. My work spans motion graphics, 2D/3D visual design, illustration, branding, and interactive prototypes. I combine visual storytelling with precise, concept-led design to create work that is striking, functional, and aligned with each project's communication goals.

I'm currently studying Digital Media at RMIT University, where I've worked on a wide range of motion, graphic, and interactive projects - some of which were selected for RMIT's Streamline showcase and the Beyond 1&0 exhibition. I also take on freelance work, collaborating with clients and teams to refine ideas, build strong visual direction, and deliver solutions that are both effective and memorable.

Whether it's shaping a visual identity, designing motion content, or developing digital experiences, I focus on understanding the brief, communicating clearly with collaborators, and producing outcomes with clarity and impact.

I'm open to collaborations and freelance work—feel free to reach out.
