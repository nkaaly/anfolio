CONCERT PIECE
"Concert Piece" is a 2D motion graphics animation that reimagines performance as an act of self-liberation rather than spectacle. Instead of seeking approval, the performer hides until the audience leaves, choosing to play purely for personal joy. Set in a concert hall—a symbol of societal expectation—the story unfolds in two parts: first, a ballerina constrained by conformity; then, her liberation into a world of uninhibited self-expression.
Individual project

Year: 2024
