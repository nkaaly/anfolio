Beyond The Compliment
This short film is a remake of As Good as It Gets (1997). It follows A middle-aged writer with obsessive-compulsive disorder and a single mother are unexpectedly drawn into a strange romance, where clumsiness and wounds gradually turn into healing, even though neither of them has ever believed in love. Created by the talented Digital Film & Video students.
Featuring Chi Bao, Ngoc Hung
Directed by Vu Hoang
