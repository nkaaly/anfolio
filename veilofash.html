Veil of Ash
“Veil of Ash” is a 3D animated short blending poetic tragedy with East Asian visual traditions. It follows a lone swordsman searching for the truth behind his brother’s disappearance during a violent, long-forgotten raid. His encounter with a fortune teller spirals into a fateful duel, exposing a twisted truth buried beneath the ashes. The film is shaped by stylized brushstroke textures and cinematic framing.
Team project : Anna/Ennu, Uyen Phuong, Nhat Minh

Role
• Characters design & modeling
• Rigging and animating
• Editing and compositing

Year: 2025
