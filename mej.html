Me.J
Me.J is a conceptual gesture-based music mixing platform designed as part of a freemium service model. It enables users to control and shape sound through intuitive body movements, offering an expressive experience for DJs, producers, and performers. The project explores accessible, playful music interaction through real-time audio–visual feedback.
